Help & User Guide

Welcome to the Citizen Grievance Management Portal. This platform allows citizens to report civic issues and track their resolution status easily.

How to Submit a Complaint

  • Go to the User Dashboard.
  • Enter complaint details such as title, category, description, and location.
  • Upload an image if required.
  • Click the Submit Complaint button.
  • You will receive a complaint ID for tracking.

How to Track Complaint Status

  • Log in to the portal.
  • Enter your complaint ID in the tracking section.
  • You can view status updates like Submitted, Under Review, In Progress, or Resolved.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What types of complaints can be submitted?
A: Road damage, water supply issues, electricity problems, garbage overflow, and public safety concerns.
Q: How long does it take to resolve a complaint?
A: Resolution time depends on the type of issue and department responsible.
Q: Can I update my complaint after submission?
A: No. However, you can contact the administrator for further assistance.
