About Our Portal

The Citizen Grievance Management Portal is a digital platform designed to help citizens report and track civic issues efficiently. It acts as a bridge between citizens and government authorities to improve communication and ensure timely resolution of problems.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a transparent, simple, and effective system where citizens can raise complaints related to public services such as roads, water supply, electricity, waste management, and public safety.

Key Features

  • Easy complaint submission
  • Real-time complaint tracking
  • Admin monitoring and management
  • Officer-based issue resolution
  • Improved communication between citizens and authorities

How It Works

Citizens submit complaints through the portal. The complaints are then reviewed by administrators and assigned to the appropriate officers. Officers investigate the issue and update the status until the complaint is resolved.
