Empowering Citizens to Report Civic Issues

Our platform helps citizens report public problems such as road damage, garbage overflow, electricity failure, and water supply issues. Authorities review and resolve complaints quickly to improve city services.


About This Portal

The Citizen Grievance Management Portal is designed to improve communication between citizens and government authorities. Through this system, users can register complaints, track their status, and receive updates until the issue is resolved. The system is developed and maintained by Keerthi Pvt Ltd.
